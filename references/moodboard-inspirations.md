## Key design patterns to steal:
1. **Personal greeting** → "Welcome back, Mithun" or contextual header
2. **Natural language** → "You have 3 apps, 12 scheduled jobs today"
3. **White card on paper** → floating cards, heavy shadows, rounded
4. **Segmented pill inputs** → suggestions as separate pill chips above textarea
5. **Step indicator** → "Step 1 of 4" subtle progress cue
6. **Editorial typography** → large bold number + smaller descriptor pairs
7. **Colored icon on white** → semantic status without background fill
8. **Minimal toolbar** → just the essentials, icon-only secondary actions
